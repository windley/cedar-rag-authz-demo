# Q3 Planning Document

## Overview
This document outlines the strategic plan for Q3 2024, focusing on product development and customer engagement initiatives.

## Key Objectives
1. Launch new feature set for enterprise customers
2. Improve customer satisfaction scores by 15%
3. Expand into two new geographic markets

## Product Development
The engineering team will prioritize:
- Enhanced security features
- Performance optimizations
- Mobile app improvements

## Customer Engagement
We will focus on:
- Quarterly business reviews with key accounts
- Customer feedback integration
- Support response time improvements
